Piano Log

Everything piano

04/07/2022

  • Musical theory should always be a few steps ahead of the practical.
  • Secondary Dominant- when you build a V on top of a V.
  • Symbol DD.
  • Equivalent to a major II in the given tonality.
  • Harmonic major flattens the VI, melodic major flattens the VI and VII on the way up, natural on the way down.
  • Minor melodic and harmonics sharpen.
  • Double harmonic major - flatten II and VI.
  • Double harmonic minor - sharpen IV and VII.
  • VII# - symbol for harmonic minor.
